A mod by Bin & Barg (trailer here!) Available now in the mod launcher!

Over 25 bugs added to the game to completely change the speedrun - think you can find them all?

Bugged Speedrun.com leaderboards here

The input fields below are provided so you can name the bugs as you find them (note that the website won't remember or verify your answers).
We also made a little crossword puzzle out of this, which will verify your answers (at least against the names we came up with) - you can check that out here!
Each "skip" in this list required some unique code modifications to make it possible. Other skips may also be possible simply by utilizing some of the "common tech", but those are not counted in this list.
Also note that it may be possible to "softlock" yourself out of some bugs, so you may want to create safety saves when you first enter a level.
